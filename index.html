Prepay is a simpler version of Splitwise: your group pays into a shared pot upfront, spends from it, and any leftover money gets split back to everyone proportionally.

No spam. Just launch updates. You can reply directly to my email.
  • No chasing people — everyone is settled from the start.
  • Fair leftovers — any remaining balance is refunded proportionally.
  • Best for trips, dinners, events, house shares.

How it works

1) Create a group pot for a trip / event / shared spend.
2) Everyone contributes upfront (any amount or suggested split).
3) Pay expenses from the pot as you go.
4) Leftover balance gets refunded proportionally — automatically.

Why people want this

✔️ Clear budget • ✔️ Fewer awkward reminders • ✔️ Cleaner refunds
