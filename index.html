Prepay

A simpler way to split group expenses — everyone pays upfront.

Prepay is like Splitwise, but instead of settling up later, everyone contributes money before the expense happens.

Once the group expense is finished, any leftover money is split back to everyone proportionally.

How it works

1. Create a group expense (trip, dinner, event, shared fund)
2. Everyone contributes upfront
3. Pay expenses from the shared pool
4. Leftover money is automatically split back

Why Prepay

No chasing people for money. No awkward reminders. No uneven repayments. Everyone is settled from the start.

Prepay is currently in development.
