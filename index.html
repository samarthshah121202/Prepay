Prepay is a group spending pot for trips, dinners, and shared bills. Everyone contributes upfront, you spend from the pot, and any remaining balance is refunded proportionally.

App preview

No spam. Just launch updates. You can reply directly to submissions.
  • No chasing people — everyone is settled from the start.
  • Fair leftovers — remaining balance refunded proportionally.
  • Great for trips, dinners, events, house shares.

How it works

1) Create a pot for a trip / event / shared spend.
2) Everyone contributes upfront.
3) Pay expenses from the pot.
4) Leftover balance is refunded proportionally.

Example

4 friends put in £50 each (£200). You spend £150.
£50 leftover gets refunded based on how much each person put in.
