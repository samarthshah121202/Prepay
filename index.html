How it works

1 Step 1

Create a pot

Start a pot for a trip, dinner, event, or household bills. Invite your friends and set the currency.

2 Step 2

Everyone contributes upfront

Each person pays into the pot before spending begins. No chasing - everything is settled from the start.

3 Step 3

Spend together

Track spend from the shared balance. Everyone can see what’s left in real time.

4 Step 4

Leftovers refunded proportionally

When you’re done, any remaining money is split back fairly based on how much each person contributed.
